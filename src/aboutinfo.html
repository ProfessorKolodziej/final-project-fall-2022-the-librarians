Banned Books Week is an annual event advocating for the freedom to read and opposing censorship. Librarians, booksellers, publishers, and readers come together to promote access to diverse literary viewpoints and to highlight the issues surrounding book banning. The event's core mission is to raise awareness about censorship and encourage individuals to uphold their right to access information freely. Visit bannedbooksweek.org to learn more about their mission.

PEN America is a nonprofit organization dedicated to defending and promoting free expression in the U.S. and globally. It aims to unite writers and allies to support human rights and open discourse, focusing on protecting free speech, supporting persecuted writers and journalists, and promoting diverse voices in literature and journalism. Through various programs and initiatives, PEN America challenges censorship and advocates for press freedom, emphasizing the role of free expression in a democratic society. Visit pen.org to learn more about their mission.
